About Me

Hi I'm Daniel and this is my little corner of the internet. I'm a beginner programmer self-taught. I hope to show my journey in learning with this website. I'm also a amateur photographer with a antique camera collection that i love to talk about. I'm also chronically ill/disabled with hEDS and POTS and have found a strong interest in coding as it gives me something to do where I feel like I'm able to progress and make something and has helped to give me a productive creative outlet.
