Supernatural is one of the most important shows to me as it was my favorite show during some of my hardest most depressed years. I've always kind of identified with Dean as a character with how he's the oldest child and felt like his younger brother needed him to step up in place of their parents, even as adults he felt responsible. Another character I loved was Bobby who I wished I had an adult like growing up, someone to keep my parents in check when they went too far, someone who had shitty parents and made it out. Someone who against what he had thought would happen if he had kids of his own helped raise Sam and Dean to be better people than had it been just John having an impact.
